Privacy Policy – Receipts (Beta)

This Privacy Policy describes how the Receipts app, currently in beta testing, collects, uses, and protects user data submitted through the TestFlight program.

Data Collected

The app collects the following information during use:

  • Purchase receipt data (e.g., amount, date, category)
  • Basic technical data, such as device information and diagnostics

Purpose of Data Collection

Data is collected solely to improve the app, fix bugs, and provide new features based on user feedback.

Third-Party Services

Receipt data is stored and managed using Airtable, a cloud-based service that complies with international security standards.

Data Retention

Data is retained for the duration of the beta period and will be deleted upon user request or at the end of the testing program.

User Rights

At any time, you can request the following:

  • Access to your data
  • Correction or deletion of your data
  • More information about how your data is processed

For any request, contact: veronadavide@icloud.com

Policy Updates

This Privacy Policy may be updated in the future. Changes will be posted on this page.
